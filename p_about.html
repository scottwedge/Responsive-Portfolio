About

You may think I'm Shane Koehler, but you're wrong. I am a man with no name. You see my picture to the left? Is it woman? Is it man? It does not matter for this person is no one.

This no one may care who you are or where you come from. They may not sleep, as sleep is for the fallen. They may end you with a single flick of the wrist, through an angry brow or a weeping sadness. It does not matter. We are no one, you cannot judge the likes of no one as the Many-Faced God does. We execute your fate as He commands.

Who is He? You dare not ask for he is after us all. Seek him and give a name and you shall drink from the fountain. The fountain shall do many-faced things that one can only find through a name given. Confused? Fear not! For the Many-Faced God is no one, too.
